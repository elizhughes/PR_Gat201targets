Plate Reader 7: 20230519

Test WT KN99-alpha and GAT201 deletion mutant 003(Madhani), Gat204 deletion mutant, LIV3 deletion mutant and BLP1 deletion mutant in RPMI at 37 degrees. 3 Bioreps, 3 Techreps each. Test CNAG_05812 (K:H antiporter) and CNAG_01983 (RCV1), both GAT201/Gat204 targets. RPMI minus Phenol Red. Grow 5ml culture from colony (1 colony = 1 Biorep) in YPD, 30C, 180 rpm ON. Seed at OD 600nm = 0.2 , 200 ul per well. Run for 2 days (166 cycles).

Calculate median OD for blank wells for each growth medium RPMI in first hour only

For time < 1hr (1/ 24 days)

blank_OD_summary <- annotated %>%
  filter(is.na(Strain), Time < 1/24) %>%
  
  group_by(Media) %>%
  summarise(OD_median=median(OD595),
            OD_mean=mean(OD595),
            OD_max=max(OD595),
            OD_min=min(OD595))
print(blank_OD_summary)

Subtract blank OD to make corrected OD and Plot OD_corrected v Time (hrs) RPMI

normalisedOD <- annotated %>%
  left_join(blank_OD_summary, by="Media") %>%
  mutate(OD_corrected = OD595 - OD_median)

Plot OD all RPMI

ggplot(data=normalisedOD %>%
                      filter( !is.na(Strain) ), 
                      aes(x=Time, y=OD_corrected, color=Strain, group = Well)) +
