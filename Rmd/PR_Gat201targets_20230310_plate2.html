Plate Reader 2: 20230310

Test WT KN99-alpha and GAT201 deletion mutant 003(Madhani), ECM2201 deletion mutant, MEP1 deletion mutant and PDR802 deletion mutant in RPMI at 37 degrees. 4 Bioreps, 3 Techreps each. Grow 5ml culture from colony (1 colony = 1 Biorep) in YPD, 30C, 180 rpm ON. Seed at OD 600nm = 0.2 , 200 ul per well. Run for 2 days (320 cycles).

Calculate median OD for blank wells for each growth medium RPMI

For time < 1hr (1/ 24 days)

blank_OD_summary <- annotated %>%
   filter(is.na(Strain), Time < 1/24) %>%
   group_by(Media) %>%
  summarise(OD_median=median(OD595),
            OD_mean=mean(OD595),
            OD_max=max(OD595),
            OD_min=min(OD595))
print(blank_OD_summary)

Subtract blank OD to make corrected OD and Plot OD_corrected v Time (hrs) RPMI

normalisedOD <- annotated %>%
  left_join(blank_OD_summary, by="Media") %>%
  mutate(OD_corrected = OD595 - OD_median)

Plot OD all RPMI

ggplot(data=normalisedOD %>%
                      filter( !is.na(Strain) ), 
                      aes(x=Time, y=OD_corrected, color=Strain, group = Well)) + 
       geom_line() + 
       scale_y_continuous(limits=c(0,0.15),expand=c(0,0))+
       facet_wrap(Strain ~ .)+
  
       labs(x = "Time(Hrs)",
            y = "Absorbance (595nm)") +
       theme(text = element_text(size = 20))+
       theme_bw()+
       scale_color_brewer(palette="Set1")
